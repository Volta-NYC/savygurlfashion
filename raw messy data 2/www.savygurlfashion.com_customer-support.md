FAQS

Do you provide International shipping?

Yes, but currently  only to CANADA.

How can I track my order?

Tracking Information is sent via email, to the email address provided at the time of purchase.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept most major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay and AfterPay.

​

How long will my order take to ship?

​

All orders are processed/shipped within 1-2 business days(which excludes weekends/holidays) upon receipt.

​

How do I know which size to get?

Product size information varies between styles and can always be found in the product description. You can always refer to our size chart. If you still are unsure and need help contact us .

How can I keep track of my loyalty rewards?

In order to earn and keep track of loyalty points, you must create an account, and remember to sign in at the time of each purchase.

Can I pick up my order?

Unfortunately, at the moment we do not allow pick up service.
