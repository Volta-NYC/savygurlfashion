Privacy Policy

​

Savygurlfashion LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, services, and mobile messaging program.

​

### 1\. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to process your purchase and enhance your experience. This may include:

*   Personal Information: Name, billing/shipping address, email address, and phone number.
    
*   Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through third-party providers).
    
*   Order Details: Purchase history and transaction details.
    

We do not sell or redistribute your personal information to third parties.

​

### 2\. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

*   Process and fulfill orders.
    
*   Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and customer support messages.
    
*   Provide promotional offers, discounts, and marketing communications (with your consent).
    
*   Improve our website and shopping experience.
    

If you prefer not to receive marketing messages, you can opt-out at any time (see below).

### 3\. Mobile Messaging Terms & Conditions

By opting into our SMS/text messaging service, you agree to receive recurring messages from Savygurlfashion LLC, including:

*   Service-related updates (e.g., order confirmations, account alerts).
    
*   Promotional messages (e.g., special offers, cart reminders).
    

Your Consent:

*   Enrollment is voluntary and not required for purchases.
    
*   Message and data rates may apply.
    
*   Message frequency varies..
    

For support, text HELP to the same number or email [savygurlfashion@gmail.com](mailto:savygurlfashion@gmail.com)
.

Note: Wireless carriers are not responsible for delayed or undelivered messages.

### 4\. Data Security & Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. While we strive to ensure data safety, no online transmission is 100% secure.

### 5\. Third-Party Services & Payment Processing

We may use third-party services (e.g., PayPal, After Pay, Wix Payments) to process transactions securely. These providers have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them.

### 6\. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to:

*   Enhance user experience.
    
*   Track website traffic and analytics.
    
*   Personalize marketing efforts.
    

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

### 7\. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted with a revised date at the top of this page.
